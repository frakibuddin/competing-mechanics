How We Do It

Bulleting Board

  • Car owners post any car question to the Bulletin Board.
  • Posting are private. We use first names only and mask email addresses.
  • Posting are emailed, as notifications, to all qualified participating mechanics within 20 miles of the posted zip code.
  • Notifications include a private email link allowing mechanics to respond to the owner’s questions, privately.
  • Mechanics cannot initiate a communication, they can only reply to a notification.
  • Car owners may ignore or reply to mechanic replies. No obligation.
  • If replies are few, car owners may expand their search by re-posting with a different zip code. Limit: 3 active postings at one time.
  • We reserve the right to exclude participation.
