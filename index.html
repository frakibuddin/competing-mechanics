How We Do It

Sign Up
  • Car owners post their car question to the bulletin board.
  • Posting are private. We use first names only and mask email addresses.
  • Posting are emailed, as notifications, to every qualified participating mechanics within 20 miles of the posted zip code.
  • The notifications include a private email link that allows the mechanic to respond to the car owner's comments. Mechanics cannot initiate a communication, they may only reply to car owners.
  • Notifications are limited to qualified mechanics. See repair categories.
  • Carowners may delete, respond or ignore any or all replies. No obligations
  • If the mechanic replies are few, car owners may expand their search by reposting using a different zip code. Limit to active postings is 3
  • Competing Mechanics reserves the right to exclude participation

Founder's Message

"Hello, I'm Stephanie, founder & CEO of Competing Mechanics. I created this site for car owners, like me, who know little to nothing about auto repairs. I used to dread the car repair experience. I found it intimidating. Often I'd accept an offer hearing no other quotes. I knew that wasn't smart.
